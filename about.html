About

The rapid pace of digitalization has accelerated the research in multimedia systems over the last decade. This growth in digitalization also leads to the increased amount of multimedia data over the internet which in turn brought some new challenges for efficient processing, communication, and security of these data. The increased multimedia data has also made it possible to use fast-growing machine learning techniques to solve the various problems in the multimedia research domain. There is a huge need to efficiently analyse, process, and secure the different forms of multimedia data including images, audio, video, and text, etc.

With the aim to provide participants with an in-depth picture of the state-of-the-art and newly emerging areas of multimedia systems, InterMedia (Internet of Multimedia) Research Group, LNMIIT is organizing a five-day International Workshop on Multimedia Applications (IWMA 2022) from March 14-18, 2022.


Objectives

The objectives of the workshop are:

  • To provide an in-depth picture of the state-of-the-art and emerging trends in multimedia systems research problems.
  • To benefit the participants from the research experience of renowned professionals working in the multimedia domain.
  • To impart hands-on sessions on tools and techniques used for multimedia processing and analysis.
  • To provide the knowledge of machine learning techniques to efficiently process and secure multimedia data.
